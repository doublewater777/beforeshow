# Experiments

## Fake Door Surface

Build one usable landing page with seven fake doors and a waitlist form.

Measured actions:

- Door selection.
- Countdown save.
- Setlist correction.
- Artist marking.
- Traffic interest selection.
- Main-show switching.
- Waitlist submission.

## Pass Threshold

The experiment is promising if target users with real upcoming shows submit contact info and perform at least one P0 deep behavior before or during conversion.

## Loop-Back Trigger

Loop back to wedge or promise if users:

- Do not submit contact info.
- Submit without a real upcoming show.
- Only use low-intent doors.
- Prefer generic concert information over BeforeShow-specific prep.
