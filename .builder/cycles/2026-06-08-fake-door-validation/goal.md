# Goal

Feature: BeforeShow fake-door validation surface.

Linked stage: `.builder/stages/04-fake-door.md`

Target user: users with a real upcoming concert or music festival who want to feel ready without turning prep into homework.

User problem: before a show, users may feel underprepared, scattered, or unsure how to start. The team needs to know which prep behaviors are strong enough to build.

Desired behavior change: users click a fake door, interact with a prototype surface, and leave contact information tied to a real upcoming show.

Success criteria:

- Users submit valid contact info and a real show.
- P0 doors generate deep actions, not only clicks.
- Exported local data is enough for a first research review.

Out of scope:

- Production backend.
- Real AI prediction.
- Real route search.
- Real video playback.
- Automated email sending.
